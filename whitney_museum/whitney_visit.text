This family-friendly exhibition features a giant sculpture of a balloon dog, a larger-than-life sculpture of Play-Doh, and much more! We will offer art making activities, hands-on gallery stations, and other fun ways to help families learn about Koons&rsquo;s art and process.&nbsp;</p><p><b>Free with Museum admission. No registration is required.</b></p>            </div>
